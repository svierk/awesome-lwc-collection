CustomSlider

A simple custom slider with different configuration options.
Source:

Example
<c-custom-slider
  slides-data={slides}
  custom-height="500px"
  custom-width="100%"
  auto-scroll
  hide-navigation-buttons
  hide-slide-text
  hide-slide-number
></c-custom-slider>
